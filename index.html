about

remember that box of CDs tucked away in the attic? the family holiday videos, the birthday parties on disc, the slideshow your dad burned of every christmas since '98?

aha.cd exists because those moments deserve better than gathering dust in a box nobody owns a player for anymore. we're a small team who carefully digitise your CDs into files you can actually watch, share, and keep safe — whether it's home videos, photo archives, old music collections, or whatever memories are sitting on those discs.

every disc is handled with care, cleaned if needed, and converted at the highest quality. we organise your files properly and deliver them back to you digitally — ready to share with the family group chat or save for the next generation.

services

standard rip

your CDs converted into common digital formats — ready to view, play, or share. perfect for getting everything off disc and onto your devices.

lossless rip

bit-perfect copies that preserve every detail of your original disc. for when the quality really matters — no compression, no shortcuts.

full collection

got a whole shelf? we'll digitise your entire collection with bulk pricing. pick-up and delivery included.

rescue & repair

scratched or damaged discs? we'll do our best to recover your memories with professional-grade error correction.
